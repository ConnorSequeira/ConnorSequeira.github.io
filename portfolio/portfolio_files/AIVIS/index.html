Current Progress and Goals

I have successfully designed and implemented a foundation of PLC logic for a palletizing and vision inspection system, focusing on optimizing performance by adjusting sensors, limit switches, and system parameters. These adjustments have led to a significant 95% reduction in mechanical/sensor system fault occurrence, improving overall system reliability and operational efficiency. Additionally, I created base code and flow charts that lay the groundwork for the future integration of AI into the system, ensuring scalability and adaptability as the technology evolves.

Looking forward, the goal is to further enhance the automation system by incorporating AI-driven capabilities, enabling the system to adapt to new challenges and improve operational decision-making. Efforts will focus on refining the system, optimizing machine performance, and reducing maintenance requirements, with the aim of achieving a fully autonomous, error-free production line. This work will increase throughput while ensuring long-term sustainability and cost-effectiveness.
